05 — Tokenomics

GEE Token
Utility

A deflationary BEP-20 asset powering the entire ecosystem. 100M Total Supply.

How can I earn GEE tokens? +

67% of supply is allocated to community rewards. You can earn by completing Academy courses, sharing profitable strategies, creating content, or building the community.

What are the benefits of holding GEE? +

Staking GEE grants platform dividends (50% of fund profits), access to premium AI signals, VIP social features, and governance rights in the GenesisDAO.

How does the deflationary mechanism work? +

A portion of platform profits is used to buy back and burn GEE tokens from the market, reducing total supply over time and increasing scarcity.

Is the platform secure? +

Yes. We hold a U.S. MSB license and use multi-signature cold wallets for crypto assets. All expert performance is verified on-chain for transparency.
